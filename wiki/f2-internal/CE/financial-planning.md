### Story 1.2 — Financial Planning

**Description:** Facilitate the process of creating and tracking Financial Planning.

**Acceptance Criteria**

* Provide a foundational base for the process of putting together a Financial Plan
* Support tracking of the many relationships with internal and external Resources and Clients throughout the process
* Works within TRPG object model and processes

**Design**

* **Design Overview:**
    * The Financial Planning feature is comprised of a collection of custom and standard objects that facilitate the planning, tracking and delivery of Financial Plans to Clients. The object relationships below make up the back-bone of this feature, while Lightning Web Components will be used to provide a cohesive user interface and a mix of Apex and Flow will extend the Salesforce Platforms built in functionality.
