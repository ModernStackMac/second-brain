# These 6 FREE Obsidian PLUGINS Quietly Replace $200 Worth of PAID APPS

> A breakdown of six essential Obsidian plugins that replace Notion, Todoist, Trello, Fantastical, TextExpander, and backup tools.

**Source:** [These 6 FREE Obsidian PLUGINS quietly replace $200 worth of PAID APPS](https://x.com/kanikabk/status/2042646783852229099) | **Published:** 2026-04-10 | **Author:** @kanikabk | **Tags:** learning

## Summary

@kanikabk argues that most people don't realize Obsidian's real power lies not in the core app (which is free) but in its plugin ecosystem. This post breaks down six free plugins that together replace the entire paid productivity stack: Dataview replaces Notion databases, Tasks replaces Todoist, Kanban replaces Trello, Calendar replaces Fantastical, Templater replaces TextExpander, and Obsidian Git replaces manual backups and other sync tools. The key insight is consolidation — instead of jumping between five different apps, everything lives in one vault. That means notes, tasks, planning, content, and tracking all feed the same system. For solo creators, freelancers, students, and founders, this unified approach is worth more than the software bill it avoids.

## Key Takeaways

- **Dataview:** Query-based system pulling from frontmatter, tags, links, and note properties. Create "Active Projects," "Reading Queue," "Content Ideas" pages without manual rebuilding. Replaces Notion databases for project tracking and content pipelines.
- **Tasks:** Turn Obsidian into a real to-do system with due dates, priorities, recurring rules, and filters. Key advantage: tasks stay attached to their project context instead of living in a separate app.
- **Kanban:** Visual board (Ideas → In Progress → Review → Done) that lives inside your vault alongside research and drafts. Works especially well for creators and freelancers who think visually.
- **Calendar:** Date-based navigation for daily notes. Simple but fast — no need to pay for a polished calendar app just to access daily entries.
- **Templater:** Create reusable templates with variables, dates, prompts, and logic. Automates meeting notes, content outlines, repeated workflows. Replaces TextExpander.
- **Obsidian Git:** Real version control and backup via Git instead of hoping a sync app works. Change history, restore capability, multi-device safety.
- **The real win:** Structure (Dataview) + Speed (Templater, Tasks) + Reliability (Git) = everything in one system.
- **Best for:** solo creators managing content, freelancers tracking client work, students with notes + deadlines, founders coordinating projects and tasks.
