# Contour

> AI platform for the full software implementation lifecycle, primarily Salesforce-focused.

## Overview

Contour is an AI platform built for the software implementation lifecycle — from first discovery call through execution-ready artifacts. Founded by Ivory Tang and team, based in San Francisco. Primary focus is Salesforce but applicable to AWS and other platforms.

## Key Capabilities

- **Discovery call bot** — joins meetings and suggests better questions during live client conversations based on ingested context (past transcripts, project docs)
- **Note-taker integration** — connects with Fathom, Granola, and other common note-taking tools
- **Artifact generation** — SOWs, rough orders of magnitude, resource plans, process stories
- **Traceability** — every decision stays connected throughout the engagement; links back to specific transcript moments that informed SOW sections
- **Knowledge standardization** — trains on your past transcripts to learn your question patterns and implementation methods; goal is GSI-level delivery for smaller shops

## How It Fits Mac's Stack

Fills the gap between Mac's post-call workflow (Obsidian Second Brain + Claude for SOW generation) and real-time call assistance. Current setup requires manual Obsidian lookups during conversations; Contour's discovery bot would surface relevant context automatically. More impactful at scale (frequent SOWs) than current volume.

## Status

Trial/onboarding in progress (Apr 2026). Mac sending Obsidian context via Google Drive for account setup. Ivory scheduling a setup session.
