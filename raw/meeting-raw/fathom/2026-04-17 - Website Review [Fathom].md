## Summary

### [Content distribution and publishing strategy @ 2:52](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=172)

The team discussed where content will be posted on the website: Recent Activity News and Thought Leadership sections on the landing page, with a full Insights tab serving as the content hub. They debated whether the Insights tab was duplicative and decided to make the activity/news and thought leadership categorization consistent across both the landing page (showing the three most recent items) and the full Insights page. For individual articles, they can either link directly to external sources or include custom writeups through the console. The team decided to launch quickly with direct links in Phase 1, deferring custom writeups to Phase 2/3 to accelerate go-live.

### [Expanding LinkedIn posts into published articles @ 5:20](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=320)

Speaker 2 shared 4-5 LinkedIn posts on RIA topics that were sent to Brian, asking whether to link them directly on the website. Mac suggested these posts are substantial enough to be expanded into full articles and pitched to industry publications (such as wealth solutions reports) rather than shared as simple LinkedIn links. He emphasized this aligns with Speaker 2's Chief Strategy Officer role and provides stronger thought leadership positioning. The team agreed to save these posts and develop them into fuller articles for publication in industry sources, tabling the direct LinkedIn approach.

### [Homepage value proposition copy refinement @ 8:39](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=519)

The team reviewed the opening statement: "High Metal Solution delivers AI strategy, system integration, and business transformation purpose-built for the wealth management industry." Speaker 2 noted the sentence was too long and awkward. Mac suggested breaking it into two sentences with a period after "business transformation" and removing "purpose-built" as redundant. They also confirmed the number of successful transformation engagements should be updated to match published materials (250+). Mac requested Speaker 2 provide a consolidated document listing all text changes so he can implement them efficiently.

### [Services page navigation and link consolidation @ 11:03](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=663)

The team identified excessive redundant links on the Services page: each of the six service tiles had a "Learn More" button, plus a main "Discuss Your Needs" button below—totaling 6-7 links to the same form page. They decided to remove all "Learn More" buttons from individual tiles and keep only one centered "Discuss Your Needs" button below the tile section. This simplification will reduce clutter and improve user experience by eliminating confusion about where to click.

### [Services page number color contrast fix @ 11:28](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=688)

Speaker 2 noted that the numbers on the service tiles were too light in color and didn't stand out. Mac agreed they needed to be darker to match the top border color. He attempted to adjust the color in real-time but encountered a technical issue where the system indicated the colors were the same when they appeared different. He noted he would resolve this color contrast issue separately.

### [Services page visual hierarchy and tile ordering @ 13:54](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=834)

The team discussed the ordering and visual presentation of the six service tiles. They decided to remove the 1-6 numbering entirely as it serves no purpose. For tile priority, they agreed the top three visible tiles should be: Business Transformation (the catch-all service), AI Design, and Data Strategy and Analytics. The remaining three tiles (Systems Integration, Strategic Advisory, and others) would appear below. Removing the numbers and "Learn More" buttons will condense the page significantly, allowing more tiles to display above the fold without requiring scrolling.

### [About page contact and engagement language updates @ 17:17](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=1037)

The team reviewed the "Who We Are" section and made several language updates: (1) Change "Get in Touch" button to "Engage" for consistency with brand voice and a cleaner, more technical feel; (2) Confirm the contact email should be "sales@" rather than "info@" (Malcolm confirmed this); (3) Change "Innovation form, by invitation, apply via this form" to "indicate interest" instead of "apply," since the program is invitation-only. Mac also noted he had already updated the headquarters description to say "globally" instead of "nationally," reflecting their one UK client relationship.

### [Partnership logos section review and updates @ 20:21](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=1221)

The team reviewed the partnerships section and identified needed changes: remove Enforge from the current list and add Black Diamond, Practify, and Orion. They need to obtain PNG files for these partners and have Bryce update the final partnership list in the shared drive. The team decided not to push the site live until Bryce reviews the partnership section with them to ensure accuracy. Mac estimated approximately one week of updates remain before launch.

### [Team description copy and visual alignment fixes @ 21:07](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=1267)

The team identified a terminology issue in the team description tile: the current text "Our advisors have held operating roles inside wealth management firms" is problematic because "advisors" has a specific meaning in financial services (financial advisors), and the company doesn't employ those. They changed it to "Our team is comprised of individuals that have held operating roles inside wealth management firms." Additionally, they noticed visual misalignment where some tile titles were one line and others were two lines. They decided to adjust box sizing (making them wider and shorter) and add line breaks to ensure all titles fit on one line and align properly across tiles.

### [Meeting handoff and next steps @ 23:55](https://fathom.video/calls/642162201?tab=summary&timestamp=1435)

Mac requested that Speaker 2 provide a consolidated document listing all language and design changes needed, which he will then implement. Mac confirmed he will complete all requested updates and asked the team to send notes rather than requiring his real-time presence for the remaining review. The team agreed to this approach and thanked Mac for his time.
